# 🎂 Birthday Wishes Template

A beautiful, ready-to-use collection of heartfelt birthday wishes templates. Perfect for developers, designers, and anyone who wants to send meaningful birthday messages quickly and elegantly.

## ✨ Features

- **Multiple Categories** of birthday wishes
- Heartfelt, funny, short, and formal wishes
- Easy to copy-paste format
- Personalized message templates
- Markdown & Plain text support
- Regularly updated with new wishes

## 🎉 Sample Wishes

### Heartfelt Birthday Wish
> On your special day, I wish you a year filled with love, laughter, and all the beautiful moments you deserve. May every dream of yours turn into reality. Happy Birthday! 🎂✨

### Funny Birthday Wish
> Happy Birthday! You're not getting older, you're just becoming a classic. Like fine wine... or cheese. Definitely cheese. 🧀😂

### Short & Sweet
> Happy Birthday! Wishing you a day as amazing as you are! 🎉

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Browse the `wishes/` folder
2. Choose the category that fits the person
3. Copy the wish you like
4. Personalize it if needed
5. Send with love! ❤️
